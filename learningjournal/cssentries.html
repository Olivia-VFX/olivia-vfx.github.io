The CSS Entries

My growth from HATING CSS to being chill with it

Entry 1 - the beginning

For a while CSS was one of those things i genuinely struggled with, well more like hated, the fact that i could see the output and think through logically in other languages (JS, C# etc) made the SO much easier for me, but CSS nope. Its declarative and cascading and from a logic perspective pretty much makes no sense

That being unable to know before commiting was the issue, so i went to AI (i know i know) telling it to make me what i want and the just copying and pasting, obviously it got me nowhere, but it was better than nothing

Entry 2 - Starting the fix

One day, I realised copy and pasting was going to get me nowhere when i wanted to improve and make my own stuff without ai, so i put my head down and got to work. Flexbox Froggy, CSS Battle, CSS Diner and all the other free CSS learning sites became my best friends, I grinded them for easily 4hrs and i began to learn and understand. Did it fix everything? No. Do I still ask AI questions and stare at my CSS for ages not knowing why it does/doesnot work? Yes. But its better than the start

Entry 3 - Where i am now

Today, I can just about get by without AI, I use it more as a tool now than an employee, its a helper not a maker. Im actually looking towards making more complex CSS based projects like single div art and maybe oneday a website (thats FULLY free) that teaches people to make CSS and gives hints
