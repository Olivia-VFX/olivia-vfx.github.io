Learning Journal

The messy, unpolished parts

This section isnt supposed to be clean, well edited or finished, its supposed to show the reality of making stuff, the fails, the wrong turns and the countless parts i dont understand yet. Everywhere else is supposed to be clean, well put together and pretty, here its not.

Every typo, stupid decision, near moment where I gave up its staying here, forever. Partly to show my growth and partly to tell myself that failure is okay, and important. Mistakes are the point here, so every wrong idea, bad explanation, crashout and hopeless log will document what its like to make this and everything else behind the scenes.

Why this section

Everything else I make, the videos, the projects and the articles show the results, not really the process; which ultimately is whats more important, so here my process will live. If you want to see what its like to make things and try and fail over and over, you've come to the right place.
